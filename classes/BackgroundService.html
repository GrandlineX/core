    Class BackgroundService

    A service that performs background tasks on a periodic schedule.

    This service extends CoreLoopService and automatically triggers its loop method every 120 000 milliseconds (2 minutes). The loop implementation simply delegates to the base class's CoreLoopService#next routine, ensuring that any queued background work is processed.

    Param: module

    The kernel module instance that the service will operate within.
